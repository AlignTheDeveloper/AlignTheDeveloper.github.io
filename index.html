The artifact is about my work on the website / webapp Tx2Day, which is a new way to approach therapy documentation. The webapp is focused on simplifying the documentation of visits and the hassle of scheduling for government-backed therapy businesses. While working on this project, I had the opportunity to showcase the user experience design skills I learned in my first year of GIMM in meetings with clients. This project was fun to build because I had multiple chances for client feedback while designing the face of Tx2Day and was able to incorporate what they wanted into the design while staying away from bland theming.
